Jatinga,Assam

Do Birds Suicide?

Jatinga, a village on a ridge, is located in Dima Hasao district, Assam, India. It is 330 kilometres (210 mi) south of Guwahati. The village is inhabited by about 2,500 Khasi-Pnar people and a few Assamese. Jatinga, Assam is one of the 10 Most Mysterious places in India, also called Bermuda Triangle for Birds.
